FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start learning.

Do I need prior experience to start?

No. Many of our courses are beginner-friendly and designed to take you from zero to job-ready step by step.

How does XP work?

You earn 10 XP for each lesson you complete and 100 XP when you finish a full course. Your total XP appears on your dashboard.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely. All courses are self-paced so you can study whenever it fits your schedule.

Are courses really free?

Yes. All 25 courses are free for signed-up learners. Create an account, verify your email, and enroll in up to 3 courses at a time.

How many courses can I take at once?

You can actively enroll in up to 3 courses at a time. Finish a course or leave one from My Courses to free a slot.
